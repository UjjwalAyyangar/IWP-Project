About

Student opinion is a critical force in shaping and transforming education system. Student opinion polls and surveys are regularly conducted in many colleges to analyse their feedback on the current curriculum and iterate it to make it more productive and resourceful for them. Feedback portal gives the students an opportunity for their voice to be heard and to receive feedback about their opinions. They also help guide policy by giving decision-makers impartial and unbiased information about what the student wants.
